# Data Notes

The source dataset is DfT STATS19 road safety open data.

Important constraints:

- STATS19 records reported personal-injury collisions, not all road danger.
- Damage-only collisions and unreported injuries are outside scope.
- The primary analysis row is one pedestrian casualty record.
- KSI means fatal plus serious casualties.
- Geography is a reporting and filtering layer, not a behavioural explanation.
- Vehicle shape is inferred from reviewed model-family mappings only.
